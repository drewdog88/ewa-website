Eastlake Wolfpack Association

The Eastlake Wolfpack Association (EWA) is dedicated to supporting and enhancing the athletic programs at Eastlake High School. We work closely with booster clubs, coaches, and the community to provide resources, funding, and opportunities for our student-athletes to excel both on and off the field.
