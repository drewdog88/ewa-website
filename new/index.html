Eastlake Wolfpack Association

Eastlake Wolfpack Association (EWA) is an umbrella organization for Eastlake High School booster clubs. We provide education and a forum for the clubs to communicate and coordinate efforts. EWA processes corporation matching fund donations, manages activity liability insurance, file taxes, submits annual report and charitable registrations annually with the State of Washington and runs fundraisers on behalf of our booster club members. We are a nonprofit organization recognized by the IRS as 501(c) (3) with Tax identification # 77-0616862.
