News

Stay updated with the latest news and events from the Eastlake Wolfpack Association.

Welcome to the 2024-2025 School Year

September 1, 2024

Welcome back to another exciting year of Eastlake High School athletics! The Eastlake Wolfpack Association is ready to support our student athletes and booster clubs throughout the 2024-2025 school year.

We have many exciting events planned, including fundraisers, volunteer opportunities, and community building activities. Stay tuned for updates on how you can get involved and support our programs.

Fall Sports Season Begins

August 15, 2024

The fall sports season is officially underway! Our football, soccer, volleyball, cross-country, and swimming teams are all in action. Come out and support our Wolfpack athletes as they compete this season.

Check our Resources page for game schedules and booster club information. We encourage all families to get involved with their respective booster clubs to help support our programs.

Volunteer Opportunities Available

August 10, 2024

We're always looking for dedicated volunteers to help support our programs. Whether you can spare a few hours or want to take on a more involved role, there are opportunities for everyone.

Visit our Volunteer page to express your interest in helping with concessions, fundraising, event planning, or other areas where we need support. Every volunteer makes a difference!

Spring Sports Success

June 15, 2024

Congratulations to all our spring sports teams on another successful season! Our baseball, softball, track & field, tennis, and golf teams all had outstanding performances this year.

Special recognition goes to our state qualifiers and championship participants. Your hard work and dedication inspire us all. Thank you to the coaches, parents, and booster clubs for your support throughout the season.
