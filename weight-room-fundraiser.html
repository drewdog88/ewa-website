Weight & conditioning room fundraiser

Bring Eastlake Wolves spirit back to the weight and conditioning (Wright) room through painting, logos, and Wolfpack-themed artwork.

Why we are fundraising

Following much-needed LWSD and EHS repairs and reconditioning of our Wright Room floors, we are seeking funds to complete the next chapter: bold, durable visuals that celebrate our student-athletes and the Eastlake community.

All funds raised through your generous donations will go directly to painting, logos, and Wolfpack-themed artwork for the weight and conditioning room.

The Eastlake Wolfpack Association fully supports this effort. The EWA is a nonprofit organization recognized by the IRS as 501(c)(3) with tax identification # 77-0616862; your donations are tax-deductible to the extent allowed by law.

Fundraising goal: $10,000

Donor recognition

We are offering tiered donor commemoration in the form of an acknowledgement on the wall for any donation of $1,000 or more.

Ways to give

Tell us you are interested and share donation details through our Google Form (link below). You can also donate by Zelle, credit card, or check.
