Fundraiser for Weight & Conditioning room at Eastlake High School

Bring Eastlake Wolves spirit back to the weight room through painting, logos, and Wolfpack-themed artwork.

Why we are fundraising

Following the much needed repairs of the Weight and conditioning room floors, which are being done over the summer of 2026, the LWSD has only approved those funds for the floor repair. Through your generous donations we want to bring back our true Wolfpack Spirit with new Logos, Decals, Murals and all of the inspirational visuals for our student athletes.

All funds raised through your generosity will go directly to painting, logos, and Wolfpack-themed artwork for the weight and conditioning room.

The Eastlake Wolfpack Association fully supports this effort. The EWA supports over 23 booster clubs and is a nonprofit organization recognized by the IRS as 501(c)(3) with tax identification # 77-0616862; your donations are 100% tax-deductible.

The Eastlake Wolfpack Association works in direct coordination with the Eastlake High School athletic director and the athletic department so this project aligns with school priorities, facility use, and student-athlete needs.

Fundraising goal: $10,000 by Jun 30, 2026

Donor recognition

Gifts of $1,000 or more will be honored with a permanent acknowledgement on the wall, so our community can thank you for helping bring Wolfpack spirit to this space.
